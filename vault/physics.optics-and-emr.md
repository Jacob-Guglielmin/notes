## Lensmaker's Equation
* $\frac{1}{f}=(n-1)(\frac{1}{R_1}-\frac{1}{R_2})$
* $f$: focal length
* $n$: index of refraction
* $R_1,\>R_2$: radius of curvature of sides of lens

## Snell's law
* $n_1\sin{θ_1}=n_2\sin{θ_2}$
* $n_1$: incident index
* $n_2$: refracted index
* $θ_1$: incident angle
* $θ_2$: refracted angle
